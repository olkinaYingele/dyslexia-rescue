Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Read Me Aloud (Hebrew: מקריא) is an assistive reading app designed to help children with dyslexia. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary for the app to function. The app does not require an account, does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information.

  • Photos and images — When you photograph a document or select an image from your gallery, that image is sent to Google's Gemini API solely to extract the text visible in the image. The image is not stored on any server operated by us.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following external services:

  • Google Gemini API (both iOS and Android) — Images are transmitted to Google's servers for optical character recognition (OCR). Google's own privacy policy applies to this processing: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech API (Android only) — On Android, extracted text is sent to Google's servers to generate audio. On iOS, text-to-speech is performed entirely on the device using the built-in system speech engine; no data is sent externally for this purpose.

3. Data Storage

Scanned images and their extracted text are stored only on your device (local cache). We do not operate any servers that store your data. Clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Permissions

  • Camera — Used to photograph text that the child wants to read. Photos are processed as described above and are not shared with any party other than the third-party services listed in Section 2.
  • Photo Library / Media — Used to let you select an existing image from your device's gallery.

The app does not access the microphone.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by children with dyslexia, typically with the guidance of a parent or caregiver. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data transmitted to external services are the images you explicitly choose to scan within the app. No behavioral data, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers are collected.

6. Analytics and Advertising

The app does not include any analytics SDK, crash-reporting service, or advertising network. No usage data is collected or shared.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal data on our own servers, there is no personal data for us to delete, export, or correct on your behalf. If you wish to remove all data associated with the app, simply clear the app's cache or uninstall it from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app's functionality. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact
